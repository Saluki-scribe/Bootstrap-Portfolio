About Me

Hey there! I'm Tara, and the most interesting place I've ever been is inside my own head (although Japan, London, and New Orleans were fascinating too).

From there, I've gone on hundreds of adventures, from attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry to murderering everyone at my high school prom. Some adventures... have been more fun than others.

In addition to reading a lot of books, I'm an aspiring author, avid gamer, and an artist. If it's fantasy or horror (and sometimes sci-fi) and there's a good story and characters, I'm in.

To make money, I freelance as a web copywriter and inbound marketing consultant, but writing so much for other people has left me with little writing energy for my fiction.

I'm excited to work through this coding bootcamp so I can land a rewarding job that saves more of my word power for my own projects.
